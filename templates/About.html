Text Summarizer

An internet programme called a text summarizer condenses a text to a predetermined short length. It distils a lengthy article down to its essentials. Due to time restrictions, text summarization tools are becoming more and more necessary. People are looking for ways to absorb concepts more quickly. Even text summarization tools are assisting them in determining whether or not to read a book, a research paper, or an article.

Types of Text Summarizer

For condensing a lengthy text into a shorter one, there are primarily two methods that have evolved over time..

Extraction Summarization

The extractive method is all about selecting the key words and passages from the documents. The summary is then created by combining all the key lines. Therefore, in this instance, every word and line of the summary genuinely comes from the source material that is being summarised.

Note: Extractive Summarization is used in this website.

Abstractive Summarization

The abstractive approach uses deep learning-based summarization. As a result, while maintaining consistency with the points made in the original document, it uses new terminology and phrasing. Thus, it is far more difficult than the extractive method.

Extractive summaries have been found to occasionally perform better than abstractive ones, likely because they don't need natural language production and semantic representation.
